Our Story

How we became a leading forex trading education provider

From Passion to Profession

Kalpavriksha Academy was founded in 2023 by a group of professional traders who saw a gap in quality forex education. What started as a small mentorship program has grown into a comprehensive trading academy with thousands of successful students worldwide.

Our founders combined their decades of trading experience to create a curriculum that covers everything from the basics to advanced trading strategies. We believe that with the right education, anyone can learn to trade forex successfully.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to democratize forex trading education by providing affordable, high-quality training that empowers individuals to achieve financial independence.

We envision a world where anyone, regardless of their background, can access the knowledge and tools needed to succeed in the financial markets.
